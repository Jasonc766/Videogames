All About Overwatch

WELCOME TO OVERWATCH! Overwatch is a colorful team-based shooter game starring a diverse cast of heroes. Travel the world of the future, build a team, and contest objectives in exhilarating 6v6 combat. When you enter a game of Overwatch, you'll need to choose a hero to play. Whoever you want to be, there's an Overwatch hero for you. All maps in Overwatch, let heroes do battles in diverse locations around the world.

They are 3 main types of heros that you can play: Tank heroes soak damage and shatter fortified positions, like closely grouped enemies and narrow chokepoints. Damage heroes seek out, engage, and obliterate the enemy with wide-ranging tools, abilities and play styles. Support heroes empower their allies by healing, boosting damage, and disabling foes. They are currently 31 playable heroes in Overwatch. They all have their own backstories and abilities.

All maps in Overwatch, let heroes do battles in diverse locations around the world. Every map has objectives, secrets, and strategies to explore. They are currently 28 Overwatch maps. To which there are currently 21 maps available for standard gameplay: 5 Assault maps, 6 Escort maps, 5 Hybrid maps, and 5 Control maps. These are the maps used for Quick and Competitive Play and are the biggest maps in Overwatch and 7 maps made specifically for the Arcade.

Overwatch also has 6 annuel events: Lunar New Year, Winter Wonderland, Halloween Terror, Summer Games, Anniversary, and Archives. All event alway come with new skins and limit time gamemodes. Like the Summer Games has a gamemode called Lucio Ball which is a 3v3 soccer match with Lucio's.

Overwatch has 4 main gamemodes with each one have a sub gamemode. The 4 main gamemodes are Quick Play, Competitive, Arcade, and Workshop/custom. The sub gamemodes for Quick Play and Competitive are the same: Assault is a game mode in which the attacking team must take two capture points, while the defending team attempts to prevent them until the time runs out, Control is a game mode in which two teams fight over a capture point in a best-of-two format, Escort is a game mode in which the attacking team must escort a payload to a delivery point, while the defending team attempts to prevent them until the time runs out, and Hybrid is a game mode in which combines Escort and Assault. Quick Play if different from Competitive Play in the following ways: it is based on player's overall performance,there are no strict rules placed upon those who leave the match,and there are fewer rounds. In Arcade the gamemodes are as followed Capture the Flag, Deathmatch a game mode in which eight players compete in a free-for-all,Elimination is a game mode in which players fight either alone or in teams of three or six against another group of the same size in a first-to-three format, Seasonal game modes which are the event gamemodes, and also some workshop gamemodes. Custom Game allowed players to invite friends to a private match with their own rule set. The Overwatch Workshop is an expansion of the Game Browser with the Create Game function with led coders to make incredible gamemodes.
